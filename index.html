Bio

I‘m a postgraduate student of Computer Science in Northeast Electric Power University. Before starting my postgraduate study, I had a bachelor degree of Software Engineering in Southwest University of Science and Technology.

My research interests lie at applying AI-baseed applications to leverage the quality of life. Inspired by the movie Her, I'm curious about the intimacy between human and AI, as an interdisciplinary study, it involves with natural language processing, affective computing, cognitive computing, social computing, human-computer interface, and etc. I'm looking forward to the era of general artificial intelligence.
